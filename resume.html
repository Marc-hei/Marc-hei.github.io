Hi, my name is Marc. I am 22 years old and I just finished my Bachelor's degree in Business Informatics at UZH. Now it is time to go deeper into the field of informatics, especially cyber security. I'm now starting a joint Master's degree at ETHZ and EPFL in Cyber Security!

Why did I study Business Informatics? The interdependence between the worlds of IT and Business are fascinating. While IT focuses on the technical aspects of information systems and infrastructure, Business emphasizes strategy, operations, and market dynamics. The synergy between these realms is crucial for an organization's present and future. The successful integration of IT and Business allows for innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage, showcasing the necessity of skills in both technology and strategy in the modern business environment. In my Minor (30 ECTS), I study Banking & Finance. The financial world has caught my interest in the early years of my life and I am eager to learn more about markets, risks, accounting and audit in my further studies.

Why do I want to study Cyber Security? In today's increasingly digital and interconnected world, cybersecurity has become a cornerstone of both organizational resilience and individual safety. I am deeply intrigued by the challenge of securing systems against ever-evolving threats and understanding the mechanisms behind digital vulnerabilities. Cyber Security uniquely blends technical depth with strategic thinking, offering the opportunity to protect assets, privacy, and data integrity on a global scale. I am motivated by the idea of not only defending infrastructures but also proactively contributing to safer digital ecosystems. With a strong foundation in informatics and a solid understanding of business operations, I believe I can bring a holistic approach to solving security challenges in both corporate and societal contexts.
